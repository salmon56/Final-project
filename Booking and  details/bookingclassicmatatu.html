Classic Matatus (Nganyas)

Type

Customized Classic Matatus

Service Name

Nganyas (Famous examples include Embassava and Ochieng’ Nganyas)

Capacity

14 to 33 seats

Destinations

Operates both within Nairobi and on longer routes, such as Nakuru, Kisumu, and Eldoret.

Features

  • Extravagant graffiti and airbrush artwork
  • Neon lighting and booming sound systems
  • Interior screens for music videos
  • Unique "themes" (movies, celebrities, sports)

Purpose

A cultural experience blending transport with entertainment, appealing to younger crowds and tourists.
