Safe Public Transportation Guide

Steps to Safe Public Transportation

  1. Choose a Reputable Matatu Sacco

    Opt for well-known Saccos with a good reputation for safety and punctuality.

  2. Board at Designated Stops

    Avoid boarding matatus at undesignated stops, as these may be less safe.

  3. Check for Safety Features

    Ensure the matatu has working seat belts, emergency exits, and a first-aid kit.

  4. Be Aware of Your Surroundings

    Pay attention to your belongings and avoid distractions.

  5. Trust Your Instincts

    If you feel unsafe, get off at the next stop or alert the conductor.

Additional Tips

  • Avoid carrying large amounts of cash or valuables.
  • Be cautious at night and choose well-lit routes.
  • Report any suspicious activity to the authorities.
